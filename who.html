Who are we?

We are Planet Promise, an organization founded in 2016 with a mission to
accomplish Sustainable Development Goals by 2030. The Sustainable
Development Goals(SDGs) are 17 global goals set by the United Nations to
end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure prosperity for all by 2030.
They serve as a blueprint for achieving a sustainable and equitable future.
Each year we focus on a different goal and do our best to accomplish the
goal. This year being the ninth year, we are focusing on SDG9.
