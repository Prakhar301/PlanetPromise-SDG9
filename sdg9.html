What is SDG9?

SDG9:

Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure

SDG 9, or Sustainable Development Goal 9, focuses on building resilient infrastructure, promoting inclusive and sustainable
industrialization, and fostering innovation. It emphasizes the importance of reliable transport, energy, and communication
systems to support economic growth and improve living standards. By encouraging innovation and modern technology,
SDG 9 helps countries develop industries that are environmentally friendly, efficient, and inclusive. This goal plays a key
role in reducing poverty, creating jobs, and ensuring that all nations can participate in global progress sustainably.
